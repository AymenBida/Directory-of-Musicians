How it works

Directory of Musicians works in four simple steps.

1- SEARCH:

It's simple, in Directory of Musicians home page, enter the name of the musician you're looking for and click on search. Alternatively, click on the search link on the top menu to view the list of all musicians.

2- LOCATE:

Get to see where the musician is located by clicking on the musician profile. It's even more exciting. You can get the directions from anywhere in the country.

3- CONTACT:

Contact the musician of your choice by clicking on the phone icon next to his name in the profile page. You can ask him/her directly for an online meeting or arrange a rendez-vous.

4- RELAX:

Once you agreed on everything. You can now relax. You can check out our blog to see what we recommend when searching for musicians
